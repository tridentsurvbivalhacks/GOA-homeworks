Story about wild animals



Wild animals are animals that live independently of humans, usually in natural environments like forests, grasslands, mountains, deserts, or oceans. They have not been domesticated (tamed) and survive by finding their own food, shelter, and mates.


Types of Wild Animals (by Habitat)

  • Forest animals – Tigers, monkeys, elephants, deer, leopards.
  • Desert animals – Camels, snakes, foxes, scorpions, lizards.
  • Grassland animals – Lions, zebras, giraffes, cheetahs, buffalo.
  • Mountain animals – Snow leopards, mountain goats, yaks, eagles.
  • Aquatic animals – Sharks, dolphins, whales, sea turtles, seals.
  • Arctic animals – Polar bears, penguins, seals, walruses.

Diet Types

  • Carnivores – Eat meat (e.g., lions, wolves, eagles).
  • Herbivores – Eat plants (e.g., elephants, deer, giraffes).
  • Omnivores – Eat both plants and meat (e.g., bears, raccoons, humans).
