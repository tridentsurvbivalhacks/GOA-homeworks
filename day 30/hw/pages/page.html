Main things to know about Naruto


Naruto is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the Hokage — the leader and strongest ninja of his village, Konohagakure (the Hidden Leaf Village).


Main character


Naruto Uzumaki

  • Orphaned at birth; parents died protecting the village.
  • Has a powerful creature called the Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama) sealed inside him.
  • Starts as an outcast but grows to be a hero.
  • Famous catchphrase: “Believe it!” (in English dub) or “Dattebayo!” (in Japanese).
