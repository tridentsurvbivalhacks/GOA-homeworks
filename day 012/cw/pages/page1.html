Sea lion

Sea lions are large marine mammals belonging to the family Otariidae, which includes eared seals. They are known for their intelligence, agility in water, and social nature. There are six extant species of sea lions, with the California sea lion (Zalophus californianus) being the most well-known due to its frequent appearances in aquariums, zoos, and marine shows.

Habitat and Distribution

Sea lions inhabit coastlines and island shores in both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.

  • Common Locations: Pacific coasts of North and South America, New Zealand, southern Australia, and some Asian coastal areas.
