  • Occupation: Ninja (Shinobi), Seventh Hokage

Early Life

Naruto Uzumaki was born during a crisis that struck the Hidden Leaf Village when the Nine-Tails Fox (Kurama) attacked. His parents, Minato Namikaze (Fourth Hokage) and Kushina Uzumaki, sacrificed their lives to seal the beast inside their newborn son, hoping he would be seen as a hero. Instead, Naruto grew up shunned by the villagers, who feared the power sealed within him. Orphaned and ostracized, he developed a mischievous personality to gain attention.
