Kobe Bean Bryant

A Biography

Full Name: Kobe Bean Bryant

Born: August 23, 1978, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA

Died: January 26, 2020, Calabasas, California, USA

Height: 6 ft 6 in (1.98 m)

Weight: 212 lb (96 kg)

Position: Shooting guard

NBA Team: Los Angeles Lakers (1996-2016)

Nickname: "Black Mamba"

Early Life and Background

Kobe Bryant was born to Joe "Jellybean" Bryant, a former NBA player, and Pamela Cox Bryant. Raised in a basketball family, Kobe spent part of his childhood in Italy where his father played professionally after retiring from the NBA. He became fluent in Italian and developed a deep appreciation for European culture and soccer.Kobe returned to the United States as a teenager and attended Lower Merion High School in Ardmore, Pennsylvania, where he quickly gained national attention for his basketball talent. He led his team to a state championship and was named the Naismith High School Player of the Year.

NBA Career

Achievements:

5x NBA Champion (2000-2002, 2009, 2010)

2x NBA Finals MVP (2009, 2010)

NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP): 2008

18x NBA All-Star

4x NBA All-Star Game MVP

2x Olympic Gold Medalist (2008, 2012)

2nd all-time leading scorer in Lakers history

Career Points: 33,643
